Welcome!

Hello! Welcome to the Non-Binaries Who Don't Fit In website! This site is used as sort of an extended desciption of the Scratch Studio.

About

Maybe you feel different than the rest, you feel like you are weird. Perhaps you feel insecure, or alone. However it may be, this studio is dedicated to non-binary people who feel like they don't fit in. This studio is a open and welcoming place where you can open up and talk about your feelings, or anything you are going through. It's okay to feel like you don't fit in, many of us feel that way sometimes, but no matter what, we're all here for you! ♥

Joining

Everyone is welcome to be active here, and everyone is welcome to curate! Even if you are not non-binary, as long as you support non-binary people, you are always welcome to hang around and chat, as well as join!
To curate, just ask over in the comments, and if we see you being kind and active around the studio, we may promote you! :)

Guidelines

When active in this studio, we kindly ask that you follow these guidelines:
  • Treat everyone respectfully and with kindness.
  • Keep arguements and debates out of the studio.
  • Do not invalidate other's feelings or experiences.
  • Try to not say things that are overly negative, overly dark, or are considerably toxic.
  • Please do not discuss anything involving suicide or self harm. Please get real-life help, or contact a hotline. (See hotlines)
